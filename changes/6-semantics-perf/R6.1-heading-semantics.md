# R6.1 — Heading semantics (h1 + h2 hierarchy)

**Status:** planned
**Files:** `src/routes/home/HomePage.tsx`, `src/routes/cubesat/CubesatPage.tsx`,
`src/routes/robotics/RoboticsPage.tsx`, `src/routes/weatherBalloon/WeatherBalloonPage.tsx`

## Spec

**Problem:** No `<h1>` on any route (measured); project pages have no heading elements at all —
their visible titles ("Robotics", "Subteams", "Meeting times and locations") are styled `<p>`.
This hurts SEO (no document title heading) and a11y (no heading outline for AT navigation).

**Approach (no visual change — keep all existing Tailwind classes):**
1. **Project pages** (cubesat, robotics, weather-balloon):
   - The hero `<p className="text-6xl font-bold">Cube Satellite|Robotics|Weather Balloon</p>`
     → `<h1 className="text-6xl font-bold">…</h1>` (the page's single h1).
   - The active section `<p className="text-5xl font-bold">Subteams</p>` and
     `<p className="text-5xl font-bold">Meeting times and locations</p>` → `<h2 …>`.
   - Do NOT touch the "Leads and Members" `<p>` — it's inside a commented-out JSX block.
2. **Home page:** the visible section titles are decorative SVG (TextHoverEffect) and stay as
   the brand design. Add a visually-hidden `<h1 className="sr-only">Space Technology Association
   of Rutgers (STAR)</h1>` at the top of `HomePage` so the page has a proper, indexable top
   heading without changing the look. (Tailwind v4 provides `sr-only`.)

Decision: home keeps SVG titles + gets an sr-only h1; project pages get visible real headings
with identical styling. No restructuring of the existing h2/h3 (ProjectCard/accordion) — those
are already real headings. Recorded; no Open Question.

State: none.
